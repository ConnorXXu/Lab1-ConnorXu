My Favorite recipe --- Wonton

Ingredients

  • Wonton skin
  • groud beef
  • green onion
  • dried small shrimps
  • 1 egg
  • Coriander
  • mustard plant
  • seaweed

Steps

  1. cut the onion and ginger, measure 15g and put them inside the beef.
  2. add 10g soil sauce, 1 egg, and 5g salt inside the meat
  3. boil the water (better be chicken soup)
  4. wrap the wonton with the meat
  5. cook the wonton. don't open the pot cover until they float over the water surface
  6. put cooked wonton in to a bowl, add MSG, vinegar, a handful of coriander, mustard plant, seaweed, and shrimps.
  7. we finish !
